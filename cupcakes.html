Step-by-Step Guide to Making Cupcakes

Follow this simple guide to bake delicious cupcakes at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate large bowl, beat the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  5. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with milk.
  7. Mix until just combined, being careful not to overmix.
  8. Fill each cupcake liner about two-thirds full with batter.
  9. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  10. Allow the cupcakes to cool in the pan for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack.

Enjoy!

Once cooled, you can frost and decorate your cupcakes as desired. Enjoy your homemade treats!
