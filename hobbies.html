How to Tie Your Shoelaces

Step-by-Step: The One-Loop (Standard Bow) Method

  1. Cross the laces over each other to form an "X."
  2. Take the bottom lace and pull it through the loop at the top of the "X."
  3. Pull both ends tightly to secure the knot.
  4. Create a small loop (bunny ear) with one lace.
  5. Wrap the other lace around the base of the bunny ear.
  6. Push the wrapping lace through the hole created during the wrap.
  7. Pull on both loops (bunny ears) to tighten the bow.

Step-by-Step: The Two-Loop (Double Bunny Ears) Method

  1. Start by crossing the laces in an "X," pulling the bottom lace through the loop and tightening it as before.
  2. Make a small loop (bunny ear) with each lace separately.
  3. Cross the two bunny ears over each other to form an "X."
  4. Pull one bunny ear through the loop at the bottom of the "X."
  5. Tighten the bow by pulling both loops outward.

Extra Tip

For added security, tie the two loops together with an additional knot. This can be helpful during sports or other activities where your shoes need to stay firmly tied.
